About This Platform

This platform is designed to streamline the management of Identity Provider (IDP) and Service Provider (SP) configurations for SAML-based authentication systems. Whether you're integrating with enterprise systems or third-party applications, our portal provides a secure and user-friendly interface to handle metadata uploads, configuration updates, and integration testing.

With this platform, you can:

  • Securely upload and manage IDP and SP metadata files
  • Validate configurations for compliance with SAML standards
  • Generate configuration reports for auditing purposes
  • Test integrations in a sandbox environment

Our goal is to make SAML integration as seamless as possible, reducing the complexity and time required for configuration management.

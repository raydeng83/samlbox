SAML IDP & SP for Your Development

About This Platform

Welcome to SAML BOX. This platform can act as both SAML Identity Provider (IDP) and Service Provider (SP).

The main goal for this platform is to provide real IDP and SP for development.

With this platform, you can:

  • Upload your own IDP and SP metadata files
  • Test integrations in a sandbox environment
  • Configure both IDP and SP for different settings and workflows

How to Use This Platform

Simply start by uploading your SAML metadata file.

  • If you want this platform to be an SP, 'Upload Your IDP Metadata' below.
  • If you want this platform to be IDP, 'Upload Your SP Metadata' below.

Once uploaded, you can access a landing page where you have a list of SAML actions available. Yet, in order for the SAML SSO to work, you will need to upload/import the metadata of this platform to your application. You can get them from below:

  • SAML BOX SP Metadata
  • SAML BOX IDP Metadata

A Shared Platform

This platform is open and shared. SAML Entity information uploaded is identified by its Entity ID. Hence, to avoid being overwritten by others, best practice is to choose a unique SAML Entity ID when uploading metadata.
